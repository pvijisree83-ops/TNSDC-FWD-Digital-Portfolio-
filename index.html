About Me

I am an entrepreneur with a passion for promoting health through food. With a deep understanding of nutrition, customer service, and business strategy, I launched Healthy Eats to bridge the gap between taste and wellness. My goal is to create a food culture that values hygiene, health, and happiness.

My Restaurant: Healthy Eats

Healthy Eats is more than a restaurant—it's a wellness-driven food destination. Established in 2000, it offers a variety of meals made from fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, free from artificial additives.

  • Founding Year: 2000
  • Location: Gandhi road, Kanchipuram
  • Core Values: Health, Hygiene, Sustainability, Community
  • Daily Customers: Serving 150+ guests per day
  • Specialties: Millet-based meals, detox drinks, vegan options

My long-term vision is to expand Healthy Eats into multiple locations across India. I plan to offer franchise opportunities and partner with health-conscious communities, gyms, and wellness centers. My mission is to make healthy, hygienic food accessible and affordable to everyone.
